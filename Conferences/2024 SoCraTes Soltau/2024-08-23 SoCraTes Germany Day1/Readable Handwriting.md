- Take your time
- Draw a frame first
- Central part of the character should be 50% of the height of the line
- Counters stay open
- Use upper and lower case
- Use pens with wedged nip
- Write big
- Use block letters
- Not much space between characters inside a word, more space between words
- When drawing lines: lift the pens on corners
